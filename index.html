About Clenomantra

Clenomantra is a next-generation Indian cleaning brand committed to redefining hygiene standards in every household. Built on the principles of quality, innovation, and trust, we manufacture powerful liquid cleaning solutions designed to deliver deep cleaning, long-lasting freshness, and superior germ protection.

Our mission is not just to clean surfaces but to create healthier living spaces. Every product is carefully formulated to balance performance with safety, ensuring it is tough on dirt but gentle for everyday use. With a vision to become a trusted name across India, Clenomantra stands for reliability, affordability, and consistent performance.
